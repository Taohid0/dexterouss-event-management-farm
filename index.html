About us

Info-1

With a view to expand in the international market, Windmill puts its footstep in the growing Nepal market in 2012 with Windmill Advantage Limited. It’s a joint collaboration of Windmill Advertising of Bangladesh and award winning Business Advantage of Nepal. Together we work for the growing need of advertising and promotion industry of multiple companies and organizations in Nepal. Currently, Windmill Advantage becomes a word of mouth in Nepalese communication industry.
